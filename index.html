About North Bay Sailing

North Bay Sailing is a small, family-owned sailing company based out of St. John's, Newfoundland. With us, you'll get the local treatment. Come see the best coastline views of the easternmost city of Canada.


Founded in 1987 by Sam and Lizzy Anderson, North Bay was created with the desire to highlight the natural beauty of Atlantic Canada. Come experience the unique beauty of the coast from our sailing vessel, no sailing experience required.

Explore with North Bay

Gorgeous, clear waters, beautiful sights, and the thrill of adventure. No matter your current skill level, we have the trip for you.


Explore the sea with North Bay Sailing, take in the unique and stunning coastline of St. John's, and enjoy all the best views and amenities that only the locals know about.
